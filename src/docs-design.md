# Design Direction (v1)

## Brainstorm (3 approaches)

### 1) **Quiet Luxury / Editorial Minimalism** (p=0.36)
- **Design movement:** Contemporary editorial + hospitality brand systems
- **Core principles:** calm whitespace, tactile materials, high-contrast typography, restrained gold accents
- **Color philosophy:** warm ivory + charcoal with *single* brass highlight for hierarchy
- **Layout paradigm:** desktop-first, structured grid with occasional asymmetric “editorial” blocks (cards that bleed into gutters)
- **Signature elements:** hairline dividers, subtle grain/noise overlay, brass “keyline” accent
- **Interaction:** decisive, crisp hover states; menus slide with weight; toasts are understated
- **Animation:** staggered content reveal, sidebar slide easing, chart/number count-up

### 2) **Neo-Brutalist Backoffice** (p=0.18)
- **Design movement:** neo-brutalism with hospitality twist
- **Core principles:** bold outlines, sharp contrast, dense information blocks
- **Color philosophy:** near-white + ink-black + one neon accent (not luxury-forward)
- **Layout paradigm:** broken grids + stacked panels
- **Signature elements:** thick borders, loud badges, punchy labels
- **Interaction:** snappy and assertive
- **Animation:** quick jumps/cuts

### 3) **Glass & Marble Futurism** (p=0.22)
- **Design movement:** translucent glass UI + premium stone textures
- **Core principles:** layered translucency, soft blur, luminous highlights
- **Color philosophy:** dark base with reflective warm highlights
- **Layout paradigm:** overlapping panels + floating sidebar
- **Signature elements:** glass cards, large glow accents, “marble” background
- **Interaction:** silky hover blur & depth shifts
- **Animation:** parallax depth, cinematic panel transitions

## Chosen direction
We will commit to **1) Quiet Luxury / Editorial Minimalism** for maximum premium feel and readability in an admin context.

## Tokens
- **Heading font:** Playfair Display (editorial)
- **Body font:** IBM Plex Sans (high legibility)
- **Accent:** Brass gold (primary)
- **Background:** Warm ivory (light) / Charcoal (dark)
